BEACH EDIT

What kind of beach day are you looking for?

01
Our favourite

Porto Limnionas

If we had to choose just one swimming spot in Zakynthos, this would be our pick. Deep blue water, dramatic rock and excellent swimming.

Good to knowAccess involves steps and uneven, steep sections. Not recommended for guests with limited mobility or anyone who finds difficult descents uncomfortable.
View on map ↗
02
Hidden favourite

Pelagaki

A small, beautiful spot with clear turquoise water and a more secluded feel.

Good to knowReaching the beach involves a significant number of steps. Keep this in mind for older guests or anyone looking for easy access.
View on map ↗
03
Natural beauty

Xigia

Small coves, striking water colour and a distinctive natural setting.

View on map ↗
04
Rocky cove

Porto Roxa

A rugged west-coast swimming spot for deep water and rocky scenery.

View on map ↗
05
Easy-going

Banana Beach

A long, organised beach for an easy day with facilities.

View on map ↗
06
Protected coast

Gerakas

A beautiful sandy beach on the Vasilikos peninsula and part of the protected turtle nesting area.

View on map ↗
07
North coast

Makris Gialos

Clear water and a rocky seabed make this a strong swimming and snorkelling choice.

View on map ↗
08
Northern escape

Schoinari

For dramatic northern scenery, deep blue water and a more rugged side of Zakynthos close to the Blue Caves.

Good to knowThis is better suited to confident swimmers and guests comfortable with rocky swimming spots. It is not a classic sandy beach day.
View on map ↗
09
Quiet favourite

Climati Beach

A small pebble cove with exceptionally clear water and a quieter feel. A lovely choice for swimming and snorkelling away from the larger organised beaches.

Good to knowThere are no typical beach facilities or rows of sunbeds. Come prepared for a simple, natural beach stop.
View on map ↗
10
Worth the effort

Plakaki

Spectacular west-coast rock formations and intensely blue water. This is one of our picks for guests looking for something wild and less conventional.

Good to knowThe descent is steep and rocky and requires proper footwear and care. We do not recommend it for older guests, limited mobility or anyone uncomfortable with difficult access.
View on map ↗
11
Natural wonder

Korakonisi

A striking rocky inlet and natural swimming spot for guests who want to see one of the island's more dramatic coastal landscapes.

Good to knowThis is an adventurous swimming spot, not a conventional beach. The route down is rocky and steep, shade is limited and access is not suitable for everyone.
View on map ↗
12
Easy sandy choice

Kalamaki Beach

A long, sandy and relaxed beach for guests looking for an easier beach day without a difficult descent.

Good to knowKalamaki is part of the protected sea turtle nesting area. Please respect marked nests, local guidance and any access restrictions in place.
View on map ↗
