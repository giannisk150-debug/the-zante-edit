BEACH EDIT

What kind of beach day are you looking for?

01
Our favourite

Porto Limnionas

If we had to choose just one swimming spot in Zakynthos, this would be our pick. Deep blue water, dramatic rock and excellent swimming.

Good to knowAccess involves steps and uneven, steep sections. Not recommended for guests with limited mobility or anyone who finds difficult descents uncomfortable.

02
Hidden favourite

Pelagaki

A small, beautiful spot with clear turquoise water and a more secluded feel.

Good to knowReaching the beach involves a significant number of steps. Keep this in mind for older guests or anyone looking for easy access.
